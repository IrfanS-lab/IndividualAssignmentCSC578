The Squid Sisters: Callie & Marie

Timeline of the Idols

  • Youth: Won the youth folk singing contest in Calamari County.
  • Debut: Moved to Inkopolis and became the hosts of Inkopolis News.
  • The Great Splatfest: Participated in the final showdown that shaped the future of the city.
  • Legacy: Continued to support the New Squidbeak Splatoon under the guidance of Cap'n Cuttlefish.

Their music blends traditional Calamari County roots with modern pop, creating a unique sound that resonates with Inklings and Octolings alike.
